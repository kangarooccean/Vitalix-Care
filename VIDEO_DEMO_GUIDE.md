# Vitalix Care - Video Demo Guide

## Quick Screen Recording Steps

**Tools Needed:**
- Loom (free), OBS, or built-in screen recorder
- 5-10 minutes recording time

## Demo Script (Follow This Order)

### **Segment 1: Landing Page (0:00-0:30)**
1. Go to: https://vitalix-care-8o6b.vercel.app
2. Show homepage with features
3. Click "EMERGENCY ALERT" button
4. Show emergency modal
5. Narrate: "One-click emergency activation with location sharing"

### **Segment 2: Sign In & Patient Portal (0:30-2:00)**
1. Click "Sign In"
2. Enter credentials (any text)
3. Show Patient Portal Dashboard
4. Narrate: "Real-time health monitoring dashboard"
5. Highlight Quick Access Grid buttons

### **Segment 3: Emergency Alert Feature (2:00-2:30)**
1. Look for "Emergency Alert" or alert button in portal
2. Click to open emergency modal
3. Show location sharing, emergency contacts
4. Narrate: "Instant provider notification with location"

### **Segment 4: AI Health Assistant (2:30-3:30)**
1. Click on "Help & Clinical Support"
2. Click "AI Health Assistant" button
3. Show modal interface
4. Type a symptom: "chest pain" or "fever"
5. Show AI response
6. Narrate: "Google Gemini AI provides instant health guidance"

### **Segment 5: Telehealth Feature (3:30-4:00)**

3. Narrate: "Easy telehealth appointment scheduling"

### **Segment 6: Secure Chat (4:00-4:30)**

2. Show chat interface
3. Narrate: "Direct communication with nursing staff"

### **Segment 7: Family Portal (4:30-5:30)**

### **Segment 8: Resources & Compliance (5:30-6:00)**

## Recording Tips

1. **Screen Resolution:** Use 1920x1080 or 1280x720
2. **Zoom:** Keep browser at 100% zoom
3. **Duration:** Target 6-8 minutes total
4. **Pacing:** Pause 2 seconds between clicks for clarity
5. **Audio:** Speak clearly, use background music (optional)
6. **Upload:** Save as MP4, upload to YouTube or Loom
